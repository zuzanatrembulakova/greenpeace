Greenpeace

Greenpeace exists because this fragile earth deserves a voice. It needs solutions. It needs change. It needs action.

Greenpeace uses non-violent creative action to pave the way towards a greener, more peaceful world, and to confront the systems that threaten our environment.

With 27 independent national/regional organisations, Greenpeace works directly with communities on the frontlines as they protect the environments they call home.
